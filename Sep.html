E-5

https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/d/d3/E-5_blaster_rifle_-_SW_Card_Trader.png/revision/latest?cb=20160710005639

The E-5 blaster was the most used blaster in the Separatists side.

It's functionalities are similar with the DC-15S.

Basic Features:

  • Small in size
  • High rate of fire
  • Low firing range

    • It's small size was perfect for droids to carry around, even when they are folded.

    It's high rate of fire, and the numbers of the droids, makes an amazing blaster rain on the target.

    This same model can be seen many times in Star Wars: Phantom Menance (Episode 1), Star Wars: Attack of the Clones (Episode 2), Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith (Episode 3).
